Human in the loop

I’m Anees. I build software, mostly developer tools and platform infrastructure, DevOps, cloud systems, and lately with a lot of LLM plumbing involved.

On the side I run tinyCrafts, a small studio for local-first developer utilities. The idea is simple: build useful tools that do one thing well, stay lightweight, and get out of the way.

These days, I’m spending a lot of time exploring the LLM space, especially RAG, agents, vector search, and how AI can fit into real engineering workflows without adding unnecessary complexity.

What really matters to me, though, is building stuff that doesn’t just work, but works well. Clean interfaces, systems you can trust, those little touches that make you think “this was built with care.”

I write here when I’m trying to make sense of what I’m building, what I’m learning, and what it really takes to ship useful software.

You can reach me at hello@anees.xyz.
