About

✋Hey there!

I’m Anees — a tinkerer, developer, and indie-maker. I’ve been building software for years now, and honestly, I love turning those “what if we could…” conversations into actual products that people can rely on.

I’m big on DevOps. I’ve gotten really into FinOps lately – there’s something satisfying about optimizing AWS costs without losing any performance. I work across the whole stack (.NET, JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Next.js) and spend a lot of time in AWS with Terraform, Docker, the usual suspects.

These days I’m pretty obsessed with the whole LLM space – vector search, RAG, agents, all that stuff. It’s wild how fast things are moving. I’m also picking up Go and Rust because, let’s be honest, when you need something to run fast and handle serious load, those languages just deliver.

What really matters to me though? Building stuff that doesn’t just work, but works well. Clean interfaces, systems you can trust, those little touches that make you think “wow, someone actually thought about this.”

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me reading about the latest in tech, catching up on podcasts, or just enjoying some downtime with friends and family.
